Become a Friend

As luck would have it, membership in MOBA -- the highly regarded institution devoted to the upliftment of spectacularly bad art -- is currently available. Actually, it's always available. And free.

What does it take to attain membership in our coveted institution? An email address and the ability to somehow tell us what it is. To sign up, fill in the information in the form below or email subscribe@museumofbadart.org with the word "subscribe" in the subject line.

Subscribe

If you are ready to dedicate your life to bad art, then become a Friend of MOBA by signing up below.

Member Benefits

Free Exhibits and Email Newsletters Members get into our free exhibitions for free. They also pay nothing to receive our coveted free MOBA News email newsletters. Also, visiting the website is free.

High-Class Events

Receive invitations to Museum Of Bad Art Events. Events are often in the Boston area but have been held at other locations in the US and around the world.

Other Immeasurable Benefits

Pretty much all MOBA benefits are priceless. Literally. But, although we are a not-for-much-profit Museum, our standards are high. We don't stoop to selling, giving away, loaning, or sharing in any way your email address.

Get a Free Pass

Admission to MOBA’s gallery in the basement of the Somerville Theater is free when you purchase a movie ticket. Schedule here.

If you don’t want to see a film, you can request a free museum pass, using this form.

Whether you are admitted with a movie ticket or a free museum pass, we urge you to support the Museum Of Bad Art with a donation of at least $5 per person. Here is a convenient link to Paypal.
